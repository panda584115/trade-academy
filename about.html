About Hatti Trading Academy

Learn the story behind our strategies and our mission to empower traders.

Our Mission

Our mission is to demystify the financial markets and provide aspiring traders with clear, actionable, and rule-based strategies. We believe that with the right education and disciplined mindset, anyone can achieve consistency in trading.

Our Vision

We envision a community of empowered, confident, and profitable traders who navigate the markets with skill and precision, free from the noise and complexity that holds many back.

Meet the Founder

With over a decade of experience in the financial markets, our founder developed the core strategies taught at HTA after years of rigorous backtesting and live trading. His passion is to shorten the learning curve for new traders and provide the mentorship he wished he had when he started.
